Notes Window
-------------

NotesWindow is a WPF Window with an InkCanvas fit to the window size. The InkCanvas allows
the user to take notes/draw on the screen. The content is saved until the 'Clear' button
is hit. NotesWindow was designed for WPFCircuitSimulatorUI to hold the user's notes while
drawing/editing/recognizing circuits.
